What is SkyCompass?

SkyCompass is a project that aims to provide users with a simple and easy to use interface to check the weather quickly and efficiently.

We offer a wide range of interactive maps and tools to help track weather data across the globe.



To check out the weather in your area, click on the "Forecast" tab at the top of the page.

Alternatively, head to the Dashboard page to access out weather mapping tools.
